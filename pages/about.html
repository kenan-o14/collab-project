Our Company

Healthy Body, Healthy Planet

Here, menstrual care meets compassion. We are not just a brand; we are a movement dedicated to revolutionizing the way women experience their periods while making a positive impact on the world. IRA takes a firm commitment to challenge every ignorant thought and foster an inclusive space for vibrant conversations on menstrual hygiene.

Our Mission

Making the World a Healthier Place

At IRA, our mission is twofold - to provide women with the highest quality organic sanitary products that promote health and well-being, and to create a better future for all through our charitable initiatives. We believe that every woman deserves access to safe and eco-friendly menstrual solutions, and we are committed to making a difference in the lives of those in need

Our special stakeholders

Leading a Sustainable Future

IRA values its customers and its partners, but there are a special few stakeholders we do not forget to value. We take our responsibility to the environment seriously. That's why all our products are crafted with love and care, using organic and biodegradable materials that are gentle on both your body and the planet.
