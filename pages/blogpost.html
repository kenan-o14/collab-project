Staining Sustainably

Some things possess an unquestionable purpose and essence, bound by the laws of nature. Some things…simply need to happen…like a woman’s monthly menstrual cycle. A process inherent to every regular woman, it comes with its own set of inconveniences and complexities, along with the amazement that the wonders of the human body brings. And the usual pads a woman most likely grew up using aren’t all as cheery as they seem in those TV ads. Underneath the “perfectly absorbent, extra large, long-winged, leak-protect, cotton comfort” are a few truths that aren’t quite as bright as those bright white pads. The manufacture and usage of these pads and tampons are now increasingly being seen as what they are under all those catchphrases - harmful to the earth and harmful to you. Pads are generally non-biodegradable as they’re made of plastics which are derived from a non-renewable source, petroleum. They are neither recyclable owing to their complex compositions. Their manufacturing process sees the use of chemical bleaching agents which again, not only release chemicals harmful to the body like dioxins but also pollute the earth. And, as we know, allergic reactions are not uncommon. Scary, right? Do not worry! Because some earth angels are hard at work, trying to replace the plastic-filled market with their gentle, organic and sustainable sanitary napkins. One such brand, IRA, is dedicated to manufacturing completely chemical-free, organic pads (link to pads) that prioritise the well-being of both users and the environment. Their tagline, 'Gentle on you, Gentle on earth', perfectly captures their commitment. These pads are made of pure cotton, sourced sustainably and aren't scented or processed with chemicals, which means you won't have to worry about any allergies or any harmful reactions. Furthermore, the improper disposal of used sanitary napkins in high population density areas contributes to the accumulation of non-biodegradable waste in landfills, exacerbating environmental concerns. The plastic stays there for decades or even centuries resisting the natural cycles of decomposition. By choosing organic pads, we can significantly reduce the amount of non-biodegradable waste we generate, taking a significant step towards a more sustainable future. When a necessity created to manage something so natural causes harm to nature herself, it isn't a pretty world. A world where our future generations are NOT going to be subject to the damage we cause is a pretty one. A world where our waste decomposes along with us is a pretty one. And our brand IRA is a step that can contribute to that pretty world. Make the conscious choice for a healthier you and a brighter future by embracing our organic pads and spreading the word!
